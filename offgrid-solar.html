Whatâ€™s offgrid solar system and how does it work?

An off-grid solar system is a standalone solar power system that is not connected to the electric grid. It is typically used in remote locations where grid electricity is not available or where it is not cost-effective to connect to the grid.

The system consists of solar panels, a battery bank, a charge controller, and an inverter. The solar panels convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity, which is then sent to the charge controller. The charge controller regulates the charging of the battery bank, ensuring that the batteries are charged to the appropriate level and preventing overcharging.
